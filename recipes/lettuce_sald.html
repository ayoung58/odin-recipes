Lettuce Salad Recipe

Description

This lettuce salad always gets lots of compliments. It's an easy recipe to make and has a pleasing taste that everyone seems to love!

Ingredients

  • Totato
  • Condiments
  • Seasonings
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Step 1:
    Gather all ingredients.
  2. Step 2:
    Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
    Cook lettuce in the boiling water, stirring occasionally,
    until tender yet firm to the bite, about 2 minutes. Rinse under cold water and drain.
  3. Step 3:
    Mix mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, mustard, salt, pepper, and lettuce together in a large bowl.
  4. Step 4:
    Stir in celery, onion, green pepper, carrot, and pimentos.
  5. Step 5:
    Refrigerate salad for at least 4 hours before serving, but preferably overnight.
